# PreMarket

PreMarket is an AI-mediated platform for negotiating business terms before a formal agreement is drafted.

It helps both sides structure their position, compare priorities, identify blockers, and move toward a clearer commercial landing zone without relying on informal back-and-forth, manual consulting, or personal bias.

## What PreMarket does

PreMarket helps users:

- Create structured business opportunities
- Share an opportunity with another party
- Review each side’s position separately
- Use AI-assisted mediation to identify overlap, risks, open questions, and possible next steps
- Track negotiation progress across multiple rounds
- Export or review structured opportunity reports

## Why it exists

Business negotiations often happen through scattered emails, calls, spreadsheets, and subjective interpretations. PreMarket gives both sides a more structured way to communicate their requirements, understand compatibility, and decide whether an opportunity is worth pursuing.

## Core principles

- Neutral analysis
- Clearer commercial alignment
- Reduced manual back-and-forth
- Confidential handling of each party’s private inputs
- Structured decision support before formal contracting
